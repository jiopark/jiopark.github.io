Summary
Hugo is a cute and friendly mascot for Concur Labs' machine learning programs.

He started out as a mascot, used to explain ML concepts and then started appearing in diagrams, presentations, graphics and more. He’s been featured in talks, blogs and materials about machine learning for Concur Labs.

He's even on the iOS app store.
