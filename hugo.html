Hugo

Summary

I created Hugo for a comic series on machine learning.

He's now a sticker on the iOS app store, downloaded over 25,000 times. He was featured on the iOS App Store Editor's Picks for East Asia

The comic series didn't work out, but those drafts gave way to a cheerful character, Hugo. Once I created him, I kept it up with Hugo for different moods, settings & occasions.

He started out as a mascot, used to explain ML concepts at Concur, and then started being used in diagrams, presentations, graphics, blogs, and has even been made into stickers and merch!
