PolicyBot

Chat bot, Conversational UX, UI


Summary
Policybot is a friendly Travel & Expense administrative bot, answering users while using machine learning (Microsoft QnA Maker) to recognize questions and answers pairs.

A user in chat (Slack) can initiate a conversation with Policybot and ask it T&E questions, such as "Can I fly first class?" If the question does not already exist in it's database, an administrator will be pinged to answer the question directly. Once the administrator answers the question, they can be prompted to add the Q&A pair to their Policybot database.

Policybot lives on two interfaces: Slack (user) and Website (admin)



Research
We interviewed travel administrators for large companies and found that there were little to no resources allotted to them to answer policy related questions.
Most users also reported that they didn't know where their travel policy documents lived, or who they could go to, to ask T&E related questions.

Process
Once initial user testing was done for Policy Bot, there was immediate feedback that administrators would like a portal or separate database to track questions. Additionally there were concerns regarding security, which is why the Policy Bot portal (website) was created. The website and slack bot have been both tested by internal users and admins for external customer including Facebook and Uber. The website can manage multiple QnA sets and uses Microsoft QnA Maker AI to recognize similar questions to already existing answers.

The UI of the website was designed in to minimize onboarding, be intuitive and minimal (to make it easy for clients to customize for branding purposes) -- as setting up the Q&A pairs would take the bulk of their time.
