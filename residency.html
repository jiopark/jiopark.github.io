Residency Portal

UX/UI, Dashboards

Summary
The Residency Dashboard is a tool that allows residents (internal employees only) at Concur to apply and work with the Labs team for up to 3 months. The Labs team provides aid (Machine learning, design, development, etc.) as the resident works on their project.

Features include

  • Admin view for managing applicants and current residents
  • Applicant view for potential residents to apply
  • Communication between applicant, applicant manager (approval required) & Labs admin
