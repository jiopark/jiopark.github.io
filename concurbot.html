Concur Expense Bot

Conversational UX


Summary
I worked on the conversational UX of the Concur Expense (Approvals) Bot for Slack and Microsoft Teams. I worked on voice & tone for the Concur Expense Bot, along with the script, flow and interactions.

Users
Users can upload their receipts to the bot to create expenses, submit expense reports and see what their upcoming trips are.

Managers In the bot managers can approve (or reject) expense reports, view upcoming trips, and manage their own expenses.

The bot is now in beta on Slack, and is in the process of being built for Microsoft Teams.
