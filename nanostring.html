Nanostring

Branding, Illustration, Visual Design

Summary
Before reworking the Nanostring brand and marketing materials, the website & brand design system was in bad shape. The style was outdated, there was a lot of demand for more collateral, and no processes in sight. Bad stock photos were rampant.

I lead the brand redesign & the new design language, which included a new color palette, fonts, icons and illustration library.

Brand Redesign
While leading the design rebranding effort, our goals were:

  • Expand artwork library to illustrations & iconography
  • Create a design system for all collateral
  • Redesign the sub-brands under Nanostring, including Prosigna, PanCancer and nCounter
  • Build an internal design agency with faster turnaround
  • Complete design documentation & reviews
