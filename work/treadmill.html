This is a UI redesign of the LifeFitness Discover

I redesigned the UI using more contemporary design principles.

Go back to see everything else!

The original UI design of the LifeFitness Discover treadmills had a convoluted interface. I wanted to redesign something that was more motivating and easier to use with fewer steps between getting on the treadmill and actually starting the workout

Early draft phases included re-arranging the data and picking out what I thought was the most important part of the UI.

After months of research, several user testing phases I redesigned a UI that was flatter, cleaner and simpler. It takes into consideration motivation, treadmill safety and ease of use.
