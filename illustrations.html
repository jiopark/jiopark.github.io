Spike Hackathon
Global Hackathon Branding & Iconography

This was an company-wide global Hackathon that took place at SAP Concur. The branding was applied to projects, presentations, shirts and posters. Over 20 teams participated in the hackathon and three winners were ultimately chosen. The 'Spike' theme centers around choosing projects that can be developed further and ultimately put into productions.

The imagery is based around the various elements of the Spike Hackathon, including VR, coffee, the Hackathon trophy & of course, code!
